understanding boxplot()¶

  • A boxplot is a standardized way of displaying the distrubution of data on a five number summary.

  • "Minimum" first quartile (Q1), median, third qartile(Q3) , and "maximum"). it can tell you about your outliers and what their values are.

  • Interquartile range(IQR): 25th to the 75th percentile.
